MENTAL HEALTH

SOLUTIONS FOR YOU

Reflect: Create a reminder for yourself to keep updated with how your feeling and to keep track of what you are while you are in action of doing things. You should also be reflecting on how your day went and how it would be better the following day by listing the things helped you throughout the day and realise what hasn’t helped to understand the cause of your unwell mental health.

Replace: Start replacing the bad things that have made you feel like it wouldn’t be much help towards your mental health and start to list down all the things that you’ve realised have been assisting you and have slowly been healing your mental health then to place them on a book or a reminder so you can keep on repeating these actions/solutions to keep up with enjoying your soon to be healthy mental health

>

Reinforce: Try to start relaxing and reducing your stress to relief yourself, find ways to be creative to keep your mind busy, connecting with other or even being in nature can help calm you down but to lift you up try to also keep up with looking after your physical health/wellbeing too which can lead to a healthy body and even improving your sleep to where your wake up feeling great.

All information given in this page is dedicated to be the soucre of useful tips and points for you. .
